A Little Bit About Myself

My name is Brian Paul and I'm studying Computer Science at Ryerson University. I am going into my fourth year of the program and plan to finish by December 2021 with a Bachelor's degree in Science. My main interests are in sports: soccer, table tennis, swimming, volleyball and badminton. Aside from that, I like to use my free time to mingle with friends, workout or polish my programming skills.

Computer science wasn't a passion of mine to begin with, but rather something that I stumbled upon accidentally. Entering this field with little knowledge of programming, it wasn't too long before I started feeling lost and felt that this program wasn't the right fit for me.

However, after sticking to the program determined to learn, I eventually started to see computer science in a new light and homework that used to seem like a chore started to feel more like a hobby.

Brian Paul
Computer Science Student
